## Introduction to Digital Forensics

### What Forensic Science Is NOT

Forensic science is not Humvees, sunglasses, and expensive suits. It is not wrapped up in sixty minutes and it involves a lot of paperwork.

### What Forensic Science IS

> Forensics is the application of science to solve a legal problem.

Key principles:

- Law and science are forever integrated
- Neither can be applied without paying homage to the other
- The best scientific evidence in the world is worthless if it is inadmissible in a court of law
